What is Theia?

Theia is a device that assists blind or visually impaired individuals by communicating the geometry and object names of your surroundings through sound

Why is Theia needed?

Theia is needed because it communicates image-data through a series of sound effects which require very little comprehension and are more informative.

Theia wearable-implementation

The majority of blind-assistant devices are unable to communicate large amounts of spatial-geometry information efficiently due to relying solely on verbal descriptions, which are limited in detail and speed

How was Theia created?

Theia runs on a Raspberry PI 4 and uses the Realsense D415 camera to recieve input. The input is then transformed into a series of sounds through algorithms we find optimal in maximizing clarity, performance, and communication-time.
